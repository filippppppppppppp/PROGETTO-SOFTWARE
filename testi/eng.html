Summary

This site deals with the most important innovators in the field of basic software and applications. After a short overview of the difference between core software and application software, they will be the greatest innovators in the field of software. Starting with Bill Gates, telling his story, his career, his school period until the announcement of Windows. After that, talk about the relationship between Steve Jobs and Stephen Wozniak which is described from their first meeting until the creation of the Apple company, without forgetting the most important innovations: the Apple I and the Macintosh. They will report on the creation of a completely open source and completely free operating system: GNU/Linux, illustrating the history of their creators, Stallman and Torvalds, until the final publication of the software. Finally we will talk about the fight related to the freedom of access to the source code of the programs and the position taken by the innovators named above.
